=== MANGLISH UNDERSTANDING GUIDE ===
This guide helps you UNDERSTAND and REPLY in Manglish (Malayalam written in English letters).
Study this carefully. When someone texts in Manglish, you must understand the meaning and reply naturally.

=== HOW TO REPLY IN MANGLISH ===
- Write Malayalam words using English letters
- Mix English words naturally where Malayalam speakers would
- Don't translate every word — use English for tech, brand names, common words
- Keep it SHORT like real texting
- Examples of good Manglish replies:
  "athu sheriya" = that's right
  "njan nokkam" = I'll check
  "ariyilla bro" = don't know bro
  "pinne parayam" = will tell later
  "ipo busy aanu" = busy right now
  "oru minute" = one minute
  "kidu idea" = awesome idea
  "njan ready" = I'm ready
  "avanu message cheyth" = he messaged
  "enthayalum nannayikkolum" = it'll be fine anyway

=== IMPORTANT CONTEXT ===
Many Manglish words have multiple spellings. People type fast and don't follow strict rules.
For example: "sheriyano" / "sheriyaano" / "sheri aano" all mean "is it correct?"
"entha" / "entha" / "enthaa" / "nthaa" all mean "what?"
Always understand the INTENT behind the message, not just the exact spelling.
